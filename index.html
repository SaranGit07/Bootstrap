What is AQI?

The Air Quality Index measures how clean or polluted your air is. It focuses on health effects you may experience within a few hours or days after breathing polluted air.

What affects AQI?

  • PM2.5: Tiny particles from smoke, vehicles (can enter bloodstream)
  • Ozone: Created when pollution cooks in sunlight (lung irritant)
  • Pollen: Makes allergy sufferers miserable
  • Wildfire smoke: Makes everything taste like campfire
Pro Tip: On bad air days, use the "recirculate" setting in your car and consider an air purifier at home!
